1 cup. butter



    1 3/4 cup. Flour

    2 tsp. salt

    1 tsp. baking soda



    1/2 cup. white sugar

    1 cup. dark brown sugar

    1 tsp. expresso powder

    2 tsp. vanilla extract

    2. eggs

    3oz. Dark chocolate

    3oz. Milk Chocolate




  • Start by browning the butter on the stove. Careful not to burn the butter! Let the butter sit out at room temperature before adding it to other ingredients.
  • Sift togther the flour, salt, and baking soda.
  • Cream togther the browned butter, white sugar, dark brown sugar, expresso powder, vanilla extract, and eggs.
  • Mix in all dry ingrediance.

  • Then Fold in the cut up chocolate and scoop into even balls.
  • Here is the hardest part! Let the cookies chill in the

  • Let the cookies chill in the refrigerator for at least an hr, then bake at 350°F for 12-13min.
  • After they are done baking let them rest for 10min then enjoy!
